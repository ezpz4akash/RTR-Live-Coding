1) Calculate the eye coordinates by multiplying position vector and model view matrix
2) Calculate the normal matrix, which is the inverse transpose of the upper left 3x3 of the model view matrix
3) Calculate transformed normals by multiplying normal vector with above normal matrix
4) Calculate light source vector by subtracting eye coordinates from light position
5) Finally calculate the diffuse light by this equation
    L = Ld * Kd * (S.N) 
    Where L is final diffuse light to be calculated
    Ld is color of diffuse light
    Kd is color of diffused material
    S is light source vector
    N is normal vector
    . is dot product

Out of above things, we are going to send 3 things to shaders : 
    1) Ld
    2) Kd
    3) light position
